Welcome to Marist College Athlone





Marist College is a boys secondary school in Athlone, County Westmeath, Ireland

The school was founded in 1884 by the Marist Brothers, A French Order, and the first principal was Brother Mungo.The original school was based in Glesson Street/ St Mary's Square. The College had a seminary for Juniorate training between 1915 and 1936 located at Bailieborough Castle, County Cavan.

In 1973, the school moved to its current location on Retreat Road.The current principal is Michael Dermody and the deputy principal is Paul Kelly.
